## Exercise Overview
The barbell bicep curl is the king of arm-building exercises, allowing for maximum loading and progressive overload. This fundamental movement builds impressive bicep mass, peak, and strength while developing crushing grip strength and forearm development.

## **Primary Muscles Targeted:**
- **Biceps Brachii** (Primary) - Both long and short heads for size and peak
- **Brachialis** - Underlying muscle that pushes biceps up for size
- **Brachioradialis** - Forearm muscle that adds to arm thickness  
- **Forearm Flexors** - Grip strength and forearm development

## Exercise Variations & Alternatives

### 1. **ğŸ“ Barbell Bicep Curl (Wide Grip)** (Outer Bicep Focus)
![Barbell Bicep Curl Wide Grip](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/275.gif)

**Equipment:** Barbell  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Biceps, Forearms

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start by standing with your feet shoulder width apart, your knees slightly bent and your abs drawn in
2. Grab a barbell with an underhand (palms up) grip as wide as you comfortably can and extend your arms fully against your thighs
3. Keeping your elbows straight, raise the bar towards your chest till your forearms touch your chest, squeezing your bicep muscle
4. Pause for moment and then return to the starting position
5. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Barbell Bicep Curl (Wide Grip)](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/275/barbell-bicep-curl-wide-grip)

### 2. **ğŸ”— Cable Bicep Curl** (Constant Tension)
![Cable Bicep Curl](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/102.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable machine with short bar attachment  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Biceps

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start by attaching a short bar to a cable pulley at the bottom of the machine then stand with your feet shoulder width apart, abs tight and knees bent slightly
2. Grab the bar with a close underhand grip, lower your arms down towards your thighs
3. By bending through your elbows, raise the bar up towards your upper chest
4. Once at the top position, squeeze your biceps and hold for a count
5. Return back to the starting position and repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Cable Bicep Curl](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/102/cable-bicep-curl)

### 3. **ğŸ›ï¸ Dumbbell Bicep Curl (Supine)** (Isolation Focus)
![Dumbbell Bicep Curl Supine](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/272.gif)

**Equipment:** Dumbbells, flat bench  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Biceps, Forearms

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start off lying on a flat bench with your head at one end and your feet planted firmly on the floor at the other end
2. Grabbing a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing in, bring your arms down to your sides, hanging off the bench
3. Slowly raise your arms up until they are level with your chest, then curl the dumbbells twisting your palms so your forearms touch your biceps
4. Hold this position for a count, isolating the bicep muscle and squeezing
5. Slowly lower your arms to the starting position
6. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Dumbbell Bicep Curl (Supine)](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/272/dumbbell-bicep-curl-supine)

### 4. **ğŸ¯ Barbell Bicep Drag Curl** (Advanced Technique)
![Barbell Bicep Drag Curl](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/364.gif)

**Equipment:** Barbell, EZ Curl Bar  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Biceps, Forearms

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. To begin this exercise; take a barbell with palms facing forward with the barbell resting at your pelvis
2. Then take the barbell and curl it towards your upper chest as in a way to "Drag" the bar up
3. Hold and squeeze the biceps tightly
4. Return the barbell back down to the starting position keeping the barbell in contact with your body
5. Repeat this exercise for as many repetitions as needed

**Jefit Reference:** [Barbell Bicep Drag Curl](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/364/barbell-bicep-drag-curl)

### 5. **ğŸ•·ï¸ Barbell Spider Curl** (Peak Contraction)
![Barbell Spider Curl](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/105.gif)

**Equipment:** Barbell, preacher bench  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Biceps

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start off by taking a preacher bench and turning it around so that you are leaning up against the angled side
2. Grab onto a barbell with a palms up close grip, then lean up against the bench and lower the bar on the flat side and fully extend your arms
3. Slowly curl the bar up, isolating the biceps, until they touch your forearms
4. Hold onto this position for a count then return back to the starting position
5. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Barbell Spider Curl](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/105/barbell-spider-curl)
