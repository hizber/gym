## Exercise Variations & Alternatives

### 1. **ğŸ‹ï¸ Dumbbell Incline Bench Press** (Upper Chest Focus)
![Dumbbell Incline Bench Press](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/31.gif)

**Equipment:** Dumbbell, Bench  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Adjust the bench to an incline position (30-45 degrees). Sit on the bench with a dumbbell in each hand resting on your thighs
2. Use your legs to help lift the dumbbells as you lie back on the bench, keeping the dumbbells close to your chest
3. Lie back on the bench with your feet flat on the floor for stability. Position the dumbbells at the sides of your chest, with your palms facing forward
4. Your elbows should be at a 90-degree angle
5. Push the dumbbells up toward the ceiling by extending your elbows and pressing the weights together until your arms are fully extended
6. Ensure your wrists remain straight and in line with your forearms. At the top of the movement, the dumbbells should be directly above your shoulders, and your arms should be nearly straight but not locked
7. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position with controlled movement. Your elbows should descend until the dumbbells are level with your chest
8. Inhale as you lower the dumbbells. Exhale as you press the dumbbells up

**Jefit Reference:** [Dumbbell Incline Bench Press](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/31/dumbbell-incline-bench-press)

### 2. **ğŸ”— Cable Incline Press** (Constant Tension)
![Cable Incline Press](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/728.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable machine, incline bench  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Chest, Shoulders

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start by laying back on an incline bench facing away from a cable machine
2. Grab two cables and hold it both hands in an overhand grip, bringing the cables to about level with your pecs and elbows bent at 90-degrees
3. Once in position, slowly press and extend your arms straight out until you feel tension in your chest, and hold for a count
4. Repeat for as many reps and sets desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Cable Incline Press](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/728/cable-incline-press)

### 3. **ğŸ’ª Dumbbell Incline One-Arm Press** (Unilateral Training)
![Dumbbell Incline One-Arm Press](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/716.gif)

**Equipment:** Dumbbell, incline bench  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Chest

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. Start off by laying down on an incline bench with your feet flat on the floor and holding a dumbbell in one hand with arm extended straight over your chest
2. Then slowly lower your arm until it is at about shoulder, keeping a 90-degree angle in your arm until you feel tension in your chest
3. Hold this position for a count then return back to the starting position
4. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**Jefit Reference:** [Dumbbell Incline One-Arm Press](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/716/dumbbell-incline-one-arm-press)
