## Exercise Overview
Rear delt flyes are essential for balanced shoulder development, posture correction, and injury prevention. This exercise targets the often-neglected posterior deltoids, creating rounded shoulders and correcting forward head posture from desk work.

- **Posterior Deltoids** (Primary) - Rear shoulder cap and definition
- **Rhomboids** - Upper back thickness and posture support
- **Middle Trapezius** - Shoulder blade retraction and stability
- **Infraspinatus** - Rotator cuff strength and shoulder health

## **Exercise Variations & Alternatives:**

### 1. **ğŸ‹ï¸ Dumbbell Reverse Fly** (Classic Free Weight)
![Dumbbell Reverse Fly](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/441.gif)

**Equipment:** Dumbbell, Incline Bench  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Shoulders, Back

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. **Setup Position:** To begin this exercise; start off lying face down on an incline bench with a dumbbell in each hand rested on the floor in front of you
2. **Movement Execution:** Then raise your arms so that they are parallel with your shoulders and hold onto this position for a few seconds
3. **Muscle Engagement:** Make sure that you are squeezing both your shoulders and back throughout the motion to maximize muscle activation
4. **Return Phase:** Return back to the starting position in a controlled manner, maintaining tension throughout the movement
5. **Repetition:** Repeat this exercise for as many repetitions as needed, focusing on form over weight

**Jefit Reference:** [Dumbbell Reverse Fly](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/441/dumbbell-reverse-fly)

### 2. **ğŸ”— Cable Reverse Fly** (Constant Tension)
![Cable Reverse Fly](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/1106.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable Machine (Dual Pulleys)  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Shoulders

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. **Cable Setup:** Start by setting up handles on two low pulley cable machines, stand in between the machines and grab the handles with opposite hands
2. **Body Position:** Bend down so that your back is parallel with the ground and bring the handles right below your chest at around knee level
3. **Movement Execution:** Then slowly extend your arms out, squeezing your shoulders so that your arms at head level and hold for a count
4. **Controlled Return:** Return back to the starting position in a smooth, controlled motion maintaining constant tension
5. **Repetition Cycle:** Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired, ensuring proper form throughout each repetition

**Jefit Reference:** [Cable Reverse Fly](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/1106/cable-reverse-fly)

### 3. **ğŸ‹ï¸ Machine Reverse Fly** (Guided Movement)
![Machine Reverse Fly](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/878.gif)

**Equipment:** Reverse Fly Machine  
**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Shoulders

**Step-by-Step Technique:**
1. **Machine Adjustment:** Start by adjusting the handles on a fly machine so that they are fully to the rear in place for reverse flys and positioning the seat so that the handles are at shoulder level
2. **Grip Position:** Grab the handles so that your hands are facing inwards for optimal muscle targeting
3. **Movement Execution:** Then pull your arms out to your sides and as far back as possible, contracting your rear deltoids throughout the motion
4. **Peak Contraction:** Keep this motion until you feel a stretch in your shoulders then hold for a count to maximize muscle activation
5. **Return Movement:** Return back to the starting position with controlled resistance
6. **Repetition:** Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired, maintaining consistent form and tempo

**Jefit Reference:** [Machine Reverse Fly](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/878/machine-reverse-fly)
