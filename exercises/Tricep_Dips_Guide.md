### 1. Machine Seated Tricep Dip
![Exercise GIF](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/220.gif)

**Equipment:** Tricep dip machine
**Difficulty:** Intermediate 
**Type:** Compound
**Primary muscles:** Triceps, shoulders

#### Step-by-Step Technique:
1. Start by adjusting the seat of the machine so that the handles are equal with your elbows
2. Grasping the handles press down with equal pressure till your arms are fully extended
3. Pause, hold for a count and then return to the starting position
4. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

### 2. Cable Tricep Pushdown (Rope)
![Exercise GIF](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/159.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable machine, rope attachment
**Difficulty:** Beginner 
**Type:** Isolation
**Primary muscles:** Triceps

#### Step-by-Step Technique:
1. Start off standing in front of a cable machine, attaching a rope to the high pulley
2. Grab the attachment with an overhand (palms down) grip
3. Keep your abs drawn in, back straight and elbows in at your sides
4. Push the rope down towards your thighs
5. As you push down towards your thighs, split the rope apart at the bottom and isolate the tricep muscle
6. Hold this position for a count and return back up to the starting position
7. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

**üîó Jefit Reference:** [Cable Tricep Pushdown (Rope)](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/159/cable-tricep-pushdown-rope)

### 3. Cable Tricep Pushdown (V-Bar)
![Exercise GIF](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/149.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable machine, V-bar attachment
**Difficulty:** Beginner 
**Type:** Isolation
**Primary muscles:** Triceps

#### Step-by-Step Technique:
1. Start by standing in front of a cable machine and attaching a v-bar attachment to a high pulley
2. Grab onto the v-bar with an overhand (palms facing down) grip
3. Keep your abs drawn in tightly and back straight as this will be your starting position
4. Making sure that you keep your elbows in at your sides, push the bar straight down towards your thighs
5. Push until you feel a stretch in your tricep muscle
6. Hold this position for a count, isolating the tricep and then return back to the start
7. You are able to place one foot in front of the other for a better stance

**üîó Jefit Reference:** [Cable Tricep Pushdown (V-Bar)](https://www.jefit.com/exercises/149/cable-tricep-pushdown-v-bar)

## üìã Programming Guidelines

### Beginner Program:
- **Sets:** 3-4
- **Reps:** 5-8 (assisted or knee-bent bench dips)
- **Progression:** Reduce assistance or extend legs further
- **Rest:** 90-120 seconds between sets
- **Focus:** Building basic strength and proper form

### Intermediate Program:
- **Sets:** 4-5
- **Reps:** 8-12
- **Weight:** Bodyweight or light added weight
- **Rest:** 2-3 minutes between sets
- **Focus:** Increasing rep range and movement quality

### Advanced Program:
- **Sets:** 4-6
- **Reps:** 10-15+ or weighted variations
- **Weight:** Added weight plates or weighted vest
- **Rest:** 3-4 minutes between sets
- **Focus:** Advanced variations and heavy loading

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Notes

- **Warm up thoroughly** - shoulder and elbow mobility is crucial
- **Don't descend too deep** - stop when shoulders are slightly below elbows
- **Keep elbows close** - flaring elbows increases injury risk
- **Control the movement** - avoid bouncing at the bottom
- **Build gradually** - progress slowly to prevent overuse injuries
- **Listen to your shoulders** - stop if you feel impingement or pain
- **Use assistance when needed** - don't compromise form for reps

## üéØ Performance Standards

### Beginner Targets:
- **Goal:** 3 sets of 5-8 bench dips with knees bent
- **Progression:** Work toward straight leg bench dips
- **Focus:** Form development and basic strength building

### Intermediate Targets:
- **Goal:** 3 sets of 8-12 full bodyweight dips
- **Progression:** Add weight or increase reps
- **Focus:** Consistent form with bodyweight resistance

### Advanced Targets:
- **Goal:** 3 sets of 12+ weighted dips or advanced variations
- **Progression:** Ring dips, heavy weighted dips, muscle-ups
- **Focus:** Advanced strength and skill development

## üîÑ Exercise Alternatives

### Easier Alternatives:

- **Overhead Tricep Extensions** - Different angle
- **Close-Grip Push-ups** - Similar muscle activation
- **Diamond Push-ups** - Tricep-focused bodyweight option
