## Purpose
Hamstring curls are an isolation exercise that target the hamstrings (biceps femoris, semitendinosus, semimembranosus) and help improve knee flexion strength, posterior chain balance, and muscular detail.

## Equipment
- Lying (prone) hamstring curl machine, seated hamstring curl machine, or cable with ankle attachment.

## Starting Position (lying machine)
- Lie face down on the bench with hips aligned to the machine pad pivot.
- Secure the lower leg pad just above the heels (around the Achilles). 
- Keep hips, torso and pelvis pressed into the bench; grip the handles lightly.
- Knees slightly off the edge so the range of motion is free.
