## Exercise Overview
The Bulgarian Split Squat is a unilateral powerhouse exercise that builds incredible leg strength, balance, and muscle mass. This single-leg movement addresses muscle imbalances, improves athletic performance, and creates devastating lower body development with minimal equipment.

ğŸ¯
## **Primary Muscles Targeted:**
- **Quadriceps** (Primary) - All four heads for massive leg development
- **Gluteus Maximus** - Glute strength, size, and hip power
- **Hamstrings** - Posterior chain strength and stability
- **Core Musculature** - Balance, stability, and anti-rotation strength
