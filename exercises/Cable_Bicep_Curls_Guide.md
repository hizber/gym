### 1. Cable Bicep Curl
![Exercise GIF](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/102.gif)

**Equipment:** Cable machine, straight bar attachment
**Difficulty:** Beginner 
**Type:** Isolation
**Primary muscles:** Biceps brachii

#### Step-by-Step Technique:
1. Start by attaching a short bar to a cable pulley at the bottom of the machine
2. Stand with your feet shoulder width apart, abs tight and knees bent slightly
3. Grab the bar with a close underhand grip, lower your arms down towards your thighs
4. By bending through your elbows, raise the bar up towards your upper chest
5. Once at the top position, squeeze your biceps and hold for a count
6. Return back to the starting position with controlled movement
7. Repeat for as many reps and sets as desired

## üìã Programming Guidelines

### Beginner Program:
- **Sets:** 3-4
- **Reps:** 12-15
- **Weight:** Light to moderate resistance
- **Rest:** 60-90 seconds between sets
- **Focus:** Learning proper form and mind-muscle connection

### Intermediate Program:
- **Sets:** 4-5
- **Reps:** 10-12
- **Weight:** Moderate to heavy resistance
- **Rest:** 90-120 seconds between sets
- **Focus:** Progressive overload and controlled tempo

### Advanced Program:
- **Sets:** 4-6
- **Reps:** 8-12 (varying techniques)
- **Weight:** Heavy resistance with perfect form
- **Rest:** 2-3 minutes between sets
- **Focus:** Advanced techniques (drop sets, rest-pause)

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Notes

- **Always warm up** with light resistance before heavy sets
- **Keep constant tension** - don't let weights slam down
- **Control the cable** - avoid jerky or rapid movements
- **Maintain proper posture** - don't lean or use body momentum
- **Keep elbows stationary** - only forearms should move
- **Use full range of motion** but avoid hyperextension
- **Start light** to master the movement pattern first
- **Check cable integrity** before use for safety
