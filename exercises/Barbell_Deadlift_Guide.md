**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Type:** Strength | **Primary:** Posterior Chain, Lower Back

**Technique:**

- Hips back, chest up, shoulders slightly in front of the bar
- Brace core, drive feet into floor, extend hips and knees together
- Keep bar close to shins/thighs; finish standing tall with hip squeeze
