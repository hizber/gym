### 1. Weight Plate Russian Twist
![Exercise GIF](https://cdn.jefit.com/assets/img/exercises/gifs/339.gif)

**Equipment:** Weight plate
**Difficulty:** Intermediate 
**Type:** Dynamic core rotation with resistance
**Primary muscles:** Abs, glutes, back

#### Step-by-Step Technique:
1. Start with your back on the floor and your feet under a bench or have a partner holding them
2. Bend your knees and lift your upper body off of the floor as if you were in a final sit up position
3. Stick your arms out in front of your chest with your hands together
4. When you are in place, twist your torso to the right while exhaling
5. Hold on and squeeze your abs for a few seconds as you reach the side
6. Move back to the starting position as you exhale
7. Back in the starting position, repeat the exercise in the alternate direction
8. Repeat this exercise for as many repetitions as needed

## üìã Programming Guidelines

### Beginner Program:
- **Sets:** 3-4
- **Reps:** 10-15 per side (20-30 total)
- **Weight:** Bodyweight only
- **Rest:** 60-90 seconds between sets
- **Focus:** Learning proper rotation and core engagement

### Intermediate Program:
- **Sets:** 4-5
- **Reps:** 15-20 per side (30-40 total)
- **Weight:** Light medicine ball (5-10 lbs)
- **Rest:** 90-120 seconds between sets
- **Focus:** Adding resistance and increasing volume

### Advanced Program:
- **Sets:** 4-6
- **Reps:** 20+ per side or timed sets (30-60 seconds)
- **Weight:** Heavier weight (10-25 lbs) or advanced variations
- **Rest:** 2-3 minutes between sets
- **Focus:** Advanced variations and high-intensity training

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Notes

- **Warm up your spine** with gentle twisting movements first
- **Control the movement** - avoid rapid, jerky rotations
- **Keep chest up** - don't round your back excessively
- **Start light** - master bodyweight before adding resistance
- **Avoid excessive range** - don't force rotation beyond comfort
- **Listen to your back** - stop if you feel pain or discomfort
- **Maintain balance** - use core strength, not momentum
- **Breathe normally** - don't hold breath during twists

## üéØ Performance Standards

### Beginner Targets:
- **Goal:** 3 sets of 20 bodyweight twists with good form
- **Focus:** Proper rotation technique and core engagement
- **Progression:** Build to 30 reps per set

### Intermediate Targets:
- **Goal:** 3 sets of 30-40 twists with light weight
- **Focus:** Adding resistance and maintaining form
- **Progression:** Increase weight or try advanced variations

### Advanced Targets:
- **Goal:** 3+ sets of 40+ reps or 60-second timed sets
- **Focus:** High volume, heavy resistance, or complex variations
- **Progression:** Sport-specific applications and advanced techniques
